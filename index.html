A little about me.

I live in the Poconos, Pennsylvania with my 2 pups and boyfriend. My journey into tech started one day when I decided I was sick of going to my boring 9-5 job. I started researching how and where to learn to code and stumbled upon Skillcrush which taught me everything I know now. I am forever grateful for Skillcrush! When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work.

I am starting to make a full switch to front-end development and visual design while learning new skills daily. I am excited to be able to use my organizational skills and eye for detail to design beautiful websites and write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to design a beautiful PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is a pixel-perfect match.
